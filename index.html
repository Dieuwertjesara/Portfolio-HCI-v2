An Introduction

I’m Dieuwertje Broersma, a 3rd year Communication and Multimedia Design Student. This is my second time following the HCI course, because the first time I wasn’t able to finish all the required parts to get a grade in time. I’ve besides that made the conscious decision to not just hand it in later at the makeup opportunity, because I felt I didn’t get the chance to get what I wanted out of the course. It fit in my curriculum schedule so I decided to just redo the whole thing.

Within this course you learn about new techniques which are useful and have some overlap with Communication and Multimedia Design in terms of potential uses for multimedia concepts and products. By learning about these things and experimenting with it we as Interaction Designers have a broader knowledge of what's out there. Because I’ve already experienced this course before I have expectations based on that. I expect most of it to be the same as last year with the biggest change being that I’m more prepared. I know based on my mistakes the first time what I can try to do differently. I still expect to run into new mistakes simply because I’m not the most organized person and that hasn’t changed. I also expect to be more assertive and to actually make sure I learn the new techniques and get to experiment as we’re supposed to do.

I've enjoyed this course a lot and am glad I've experienced it a second time since the way of learning, through experimenting and tinkering without having a specific outcome in mind , works well with me, but the first time wasn't as beneficial as it could be
